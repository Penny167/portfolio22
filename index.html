About me

I'm relatively new to web development after first studying law then spending a number of years working in financial services in London.

Progression means my roles focused increasingly on people management and as someone who thrives on solving complex logical problems, I felt intellectually frustrated! Eventually I decided to take a career break and do some soul-searching. Having worked on a number of IT projects during my career I'd always been curious about software development. Eventually I decided to take the plunge and sign up for a course in full-stack web development.

I've now been developing for just over a year and love the challenges and creativity of coding! I have a sound working knowledge of JavaScript and am excited to deepen my knowledge of JavaScript frameworks and libraries by working on interesting projects that solve real-world problems.
